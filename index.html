About Us

Founded in 2020, RecipesPinned began as a passion project by a group of food enthusiasts who noticed how difficult it was to find reliable, tested recipes online. What started as a small collection of favorite Pinterest recipes has grown into a comprehensive platform serving over 10,000 daily users.

Our mission is to make cooking accessible, enjoyable, and stress-free for everyone. We believe that good food brings people together, and that's why we're committed to providing free access to the best recipes on Pinterest. Our core values include transparency, community support, and a dedication to helping people discover the joy of cooking.
